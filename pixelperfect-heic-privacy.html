Privacy Policy — PixelPerfect: HEIC to JPG Converter

Last updated: 2026-07-05

Applies to: PixelPerfect: HEIC to JPG Converter (Microsoft Store)

Summary

PixelPerfect works fully offline. Your photos and their contents never leave your computer. We do not run servers that receive your files, we do not have accounts, and we cannot see what you convert.

What data we collect

None of your content. All image conversion happens locally on your device. Your photos, the videos extracted from Live Photos, and their EXIF metadata (including GPS location and capture date) are read and written only on your own computer. They are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared.

The application does not collect analytics, usage statistics, or telemetry, and it does not track you.

Optional connected features (off by default / user-initiated)

The core conversion feature needs no internet connection. A small number of optional features may connect to the internet, and only when you use or enable them:

  • Purchasing the full version — handled entirely by the Microsoft Store. We never see your payment details.
  • Rating the app — opens the Microsoft Store rating dialog at your request.
  • Promotional / remote configuration content — if enabled, the app may fetch promotional messages. This is optional and can be turned off in the app's Privacy settings.
  • Crash reports — if you opt in, a crash report may be sent to help us fix problems. This is opt-in and off by default.

None of these optional features transmit the photos you convert.

Data storage

Application settings (such as your chosen language, theme and default output format) and license state are stored locally on your computer under your user profile. This data stays on your device.

Children's privacy

The application is a general-purpose utility and is not directed at children. It collects no personal data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL and the "Last updated" date above will change.
